Welcome to Humbleman Site Key Manager

A PGP-encrypted credential vault that lives entirely on your device. Only holders of an authorized key can unlock it.

💡 Did you know? Humbleman uses PGP key pairs. Your private key is your secret identity — only you hold it, and it's the one thing that unlocks your vault. Your public key encrypts the vault so that only your matching private key can open it. A passphrase adds a second layer of protection on the private key at rest. Together, these three things are what keep your credentials secure.
